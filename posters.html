Our custom made posters will soon hang around our school in order to educate our peers on the truth of mental illness. We have chosen some topics that often affect adolescents such as depression, bipolar disorder and stress. Also included on the bottom of our “Depression & You” and “Mental Health & You” posters are tearable strips with the National Suicide Prevention Hotline, to lend a helping hand to those who are too nervous to ask for help. We truly hope these posters will not only help those struggling with a mental illness, but also others to be more understanding of those with such conditions

-Zach

OK2Talk

This website offers a safe space for teens and young adults living with mental illness. Many teens do not understand their thoughts and blame themselves for their mental illness. OK2TALK allows these people to reach out to each other and move forward in life.

National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)

NAMI provides support for all types of mental illness. They offer specific services based on the issue being faced.

Healthy Minds Philly

Healthy Minds Philly offers support for anyone struggling with mental illness in the Philadelphia area. They provide suicide prevention hotlines as well as online screenings for mental illnesses.

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA)

SAMHSA offers a treatment referral hotline as well as a treatment locator available on their website.

Anxiety and Depression Association of America (ADAA)

The ADAA provides support and help to those suffering from anxiety, depression, PTSD, and OCD. They offer assistance in finding therapists and finding the right treatment for you.
